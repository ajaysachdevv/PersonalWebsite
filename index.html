Algorithmic TradingDevelopment

Trading Bot

I kept making emotional trades. So I built a system that removes me from the equation. Every day at 10:35am it scans 20 FTSE 100 stocks, checks RNS news, runs technical indicators (EMA-50, VWAP, RSI-14), then Claude rates the setup and a separate Claude audits the risk. Max one position at a time. Hard cap of £750 per trade. 3% stop loss, 6% take profit. Automatic kill-switch if equity drops too far. Everything closes at 4pm, nothing held overnight. Currently paper trading.
